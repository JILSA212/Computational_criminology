Instructions

Geographic profiling is a criminal investigative methodology that analyzes the locations of a connected series of crimes to determine the most probable area of offender residence. We provide an easy way to visualize this concept with the help of technology. It is intended for both, hobbist to learn about it and professionals to use it in real case.



Using this tool is very simple, just click on "Proceed" and you will be directed to a map where you can see a map to input locations of crime scenes just by clicking on that location, you can submit that and leave the rest to us. In the end, we have also given option for you to manipulate some variables according to your availability.
